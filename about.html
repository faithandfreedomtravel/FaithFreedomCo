I'm Monte, the founder of Faith & Freedom Travel Co., where every journey is designed to inspire deeper faith, bold adventure, and meaningful connection.

As a proud United States Navy veteran, I had the opportunity to travel the world and experience the beauty of different cultures, coastlines, and destinations. Those experiences sparked a passion for travel and gave me a deep appreciation for the joy and connection that comes from exploring new places — especially with the people who matter most.

Today, my mission is helping families and couples plan stress-free cruise vacations and meaningful getaways. Whether it's a family vacation, a couples' retreat, or a bucket-list escape, I'm committed to planning trips that are seamless, memorable, and meaningful.

Faith is at the center of my business. I believe travel is a gift — an opportunity to slow down, reconnect with loved ones, and experience the beauty of God's creation together.
